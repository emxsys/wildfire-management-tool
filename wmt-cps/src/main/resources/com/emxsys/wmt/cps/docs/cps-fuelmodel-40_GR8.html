Standard Fuel Model GR8 (108)

High Load, Very Coarse, Humid Climate Grass (Dynamic)

Grass Fuel Type Models (GR)

 

Description: The primary carrier of fire in GR8 is continuous, very coarse, humidclimate grass. Load and depth are greater than GR6. Spread rate and flame length can be extreme if grass is fully cured.

Fine fuel load (t/ac) 7.8
Characteristic SAV (ft-1) 1302
Packing ratio (dimensionless) 0.00316
Extinction moisture content (percent) 30
